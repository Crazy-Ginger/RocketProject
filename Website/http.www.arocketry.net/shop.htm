Grizzly G5962 18" x 60" Precision Machine Shop Lathe
Features:

  • Distance between centers: 60"
  • Motor size (3 phase, 220V, 2 speed): 6 H.P.
  • Spindle bore: 3-1/16"
  • Spindle size/type: D1-8 camlock
  • Swing over bed: 18-1/4"

The maunfacturer describes this lathe: This lathe is loaded with industrial sized features. Combine a speed range of 24-1600 RPM with the large swing dimensions (11 1/4" clearance over the cross slide!) and you get great finishes at maximum diameters! Other important features include: a large 3 1/16" spindle bore, auto carriage stop with indexable cams, apron clutch, interlocked apron controls, hex shaft feed rod, 11 3/4" wide bed and no gear changes for inch or metric threading plus much, much more! Take a look at the standard equipment that comes with this machine and you'll agree, this is an incredible package for less than ten grand! We have not sacrificed one ounce of quality--this is one beautiful machine!
