CRC Handbook of Chemistry and Physics
CRC Press, Inc.
ISBN 0849304830
The CRC Handbook of Chemistry and Physics is an indespensable desk reference. It features a wealth of physical constants for nearly any chemical you would consider using in rocketry. Highly recommended!

The Merk Index
Merk & Co., Inc.
ISBN 0911910131
The Merk Index is a chemical dictionary. Each chemical is listed by name, with a complete index in the back cross-referencing the entries. A typical entry would include citations on synthesis, physical characteristics such as melting point, boiling point, solubility, decomposition, common uses, toxicity and much more. The Merk Index is indespensible and has earned a high recommendation.
